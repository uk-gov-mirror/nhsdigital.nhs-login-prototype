Ask your GP surgery if you are registered for their online services

People who are registered for online services can use their registration details to prove who they are.

What you need to do if you are not registered:

  1. Contact your GP surgery. If you can, contact them without visiting in person.
  2. Ask your GP surgery to register you for their online services.
  3. Tell your GP surgery that you need your registration details for their online services. The 3 details you need are:
    • Linkage Key (could be called Passphrase)
    • O.D.S. Code (could be called Organisation Code or Practice I.D.)
    • Account I.D.
  4. When you have all 3 details, use your NHS login to access the NHS App. You can use the 3 registration details to prove who you are instead of sending a photo of your I.D.

What you need to do if you are registered:

  1. Contact your GP surgery. If you can, contact them without visiting in person.
  2. Ask your GP surgery for the 3 registration details you need for their online services.
  3. When you have all 3 details, use your NHS login to access NHS App. You can use the 3 registration details to prove who you are instead of sending a photo of your I.D.
Or

You can send a photo of your I.D. to prove who you are.
